Frequently asked questions

How do I create a new note?

Press ⌃⇧N from anywhere — even while you're in another app — and a new note opens instantly. You can change this shortcut at any time in Settings.

How do I keep a note on top of other windows?

Pin any note to make it float above your other windows so it's never buried. You can also open all of your pinned notes at once from the menu bar icon.

How do I organize my notes?

Group notes into named collections — for example Projects, Ideas, Shopping, or Meeting Notes. Browse and reopen them anytime from the menu bar icon.

Where are my notes stored? Are they backed up?

Your notes are stored locally on your Mac and stay under your control. They are included in your regular Mac backups, such as Time Machine.

Can QuickSticky start automatically when I turn on my Mac?

Yes. You can enable launch at login in Settings so QuickSticky is ready in your menu bar whenever you need it.
