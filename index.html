Privacy Policy

QuickSticky · Last updated June 10, 2026

In short: QuickSticky collects nothing. It runs entirely on your Mac, makes no network connections, and your notes never leave your device.

Data we collect

QuickSticky does not collect any personal information. There are no accounts to create, no sign-in, and no analytics or tracking of any kind.

Where your notes live

Everything you write — notes, collections, checklists, and preferences — is stored locally on your Mac. This data stays under your control and is never transmitted to the developer or to anyone else.

Network connections

QuickSticky works fully offline. The app does not connect to the internet, does not send data to external servers, and does not communicate with any third party.

AI writing tools

The built-in writing assistant runs locally on your Mac. The text you write is processed on your device and is not uploaded, stored externally, or shared.

Third-party services

QuickSticky does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children

QuickSticky does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If the way the app works ever changes, this policy will be updated and the revised date above will reflect that. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.
